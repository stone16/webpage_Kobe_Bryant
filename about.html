Misson

In this project, we try to use the technique learnt from this course to visualize statistics for a specific NBA player (name to be determined). Through our survey, we found current data about NBA players are shown by data tables on most websites. It’s hard to make comparison for a specific type of data across different year and make comparison among different players. In this project, we aim to offer a more direct and cleaner way for readers to figure out how well the player is.
